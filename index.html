Launch Details

Rupee is scheduled to be launched on the UNCX network, a decentralized launchpad that provides fair and secure token sales[^5^][2]. 100,000 PKR tokens will be available for purchase at the launch, with the rest of the tokens locked in a smart contract. 100% of the sales proceeds will be used for liquidity on decentralized exchanges such as QuickSwap[^6^][3]. The launch date and time will be announced soon.

How to Buy

To buy Rupee tokens, you will need:

  1. A MetaMask wallet or any other wallet that supports Polygon network[^7^][4]
  2. Some MATIC tokens to pay for gas fees[^1^][5]
  3. Some UNCX tokens to participate in the launchpad[^2^][6]

Follow these steps to buy Rupee tokens:

  1. Connect your wallet to the Polygon network. You can use the Polygon web wallet to switch networks easily[^3^][7].
  2. Go to the UNCX website and connect your wallet[^8^][8].
  3. Find the Rupee token sale and click on "Join".
  4. Enter the amount of UNCX tokens you want to spend and confirm the transaction.
  5. Wait for the token sale to end and claim your Rupee tokens.
  6. Add Rupee token to your wallet by using the contract address and the token details above.
  7. Enjoy your Rupee tokens and support the vision of a decentralized and transparent economy.
